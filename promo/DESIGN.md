# Nyx Promo — Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Dark, premium developer-tool aesthetic: deep midnight-blue-to-black canvas with
a subtle starfield and a soft crescent-moon glow (Nyx = goddess of night).
Crisp white display type over cyan accents; real product screenshots float as
tilted glowing cards. Terminal/monospace details ground everything in
developer reality. Confident, technical, no fluff.

## Colors

- `#0e0f13` canvas base (deep night black)
- `#14161d` / `#22283a` radial depth tones
- `#eaeaea` primary text (soft white)
- `#4daafc` accent (Nyx cyan — chips, highlights, terminal prompt)
- `#9d9da8` muted secondary text
- `#3fb950` diff-green / success (sparingly)

## Typography

- Display/headlines: **Inter** (weights 700–900, tight tracking)
- Code/terminal/labels: **JetBrains Mono**

## Motion

- Medium-high energy: push-slide primary transition (0.4s power3.inOut),
  zoom-through accent for the hero reveal, dip-to-black outro
- Slow drift/zoom on screenshot cards (scale 1.0 → 1.04 over scene length)
- Entrances: y-rise + fade for text, scale-in for cards, x-slide for chips

## What NOT to Do

- No full-screen linear gradients (H.264 banding) — radial only
- No pure #fff on pure #000; keep the blue-black depth
- No rainbow palettes, no more than cyan + white + muted grey (+ green sparingly)
- No playful/bouncy easings (elastic, bounce) — this is a precision tool
- No stock-photo imagery — only real product screenshots and abstract night sky
