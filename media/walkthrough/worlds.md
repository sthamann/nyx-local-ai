# Two worlds, clearly separated

Cursor's agent panel talks to the cloud. **Nyx talks only to your own machines** — Ollama, LM Studio, or any OpenAI-compatible local endpoint.

You can tell them apart at a glance:

- Nyx shows a **✦ NYX wordmark** and an **accent line** at the top of its panel (color configurable via `nyx.accentColor`).
- The native panel header saying **"New Agent"** with a `+` belongs to **Cursor**, not Nyx. Nyx's new-chat lives in its own toolbar (**+ New**) and in the tab strip.

For the clearest separation, open Nyx as a **full editor tab**: both agents stay visible side by side, and switching worlds is just a glance left or right.
