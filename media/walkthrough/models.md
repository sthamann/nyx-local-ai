# Your hardware, your models

Nyx scans for local models on start:

- **Ollama** at `http://localhost:11434`
- **LM Studio** at `http://localhost:1234`

Every model appears in the picker with capability badges: 🔧 tools · 👁 vision · 🧠 thinking.

Have more machines on your network? Open **⚙ Manage models** in the Nyx panel and add labeled endpoints (e.g. *"DGX Cluster"*, *"Mac Studio"*). API keys are stored in the editor's secret storage — never in settings.
